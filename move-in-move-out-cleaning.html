Move Out Cleaning

Leave It Better Than You Found It.

A move-out clean is one of the most thorough services we offer. When the furniture is gone and every square inch is visible, there's nowhere to hide — and we don't try to. We clean every surface, every appliance interior, every cabinet and closet so the next person walks into something genuinely clean.

Landlords and property managers across Fairfield County request us specifically for tenant move-outs because we're reliable, thorough, and we show up when we say we will.

  • All appliance interiors — oven, refrigerator, dishwasher, microwave
  • Every cabinet and drawer interior
  • All closets and closet shelving
  • Baseboards, window sills, and tracks throughout
  • Bathrooms fully scrubbed — grout, fixtures, mirrors
  • All floors vacuumed and mopped
  • Walls spot-cleaned, switch plates and door handles wiped

Move In Cleaning

Start Fresh in Your New Space.

Even a home that looks clean when you tour it isn't necessarily clean. Previous owners leave behind grease in oven corners, residue in cabinet interiors, and buildup in places nobody thinks to check. A move-in clean from Joy Cleaning means you're starting with a genuinely sanitized home — not someone else's version of clean.

Many of our clients book a move-in clean before their furniture arrives so every inch is accessible. We work around your move-in timeline.

  • Full kitchen — appliance interiors, cabinets, counters, sink
  • All bathrooms sanitized top to bottom
  • Every room dusted, vacuumed, and mopped
  • Closets cleaned out and ready to use
  • Windows and mirrors streak-free
  • Baseboards and vents throughout
