House Cleaning

A thorough top-to-bottom clean for every room — the dependable option most of our families book.

  • Dust all surfaces, shelves, and ceiling fans
  • Vacuum and mop every floor
  • Kitchen counters, sink, and appliance exteriors
  • Clean and disinfect every bathroom
  • Empty trash, make beds, tidy common areas
