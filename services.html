Standard Cleaning

Our standard clean is the backbone of a consistently tidy home. Designed for weekly or bi-weekly visits, it keeps dust, grime, and clutter at bay so your space always feels fresh and welcoming — without any effort on your part.

  • Dust all surfaces, shelves, and ceiling fans
  • Vacuum carpets, rugs, and upholstered furniture
  • Mop all hard floors with appropriate solutions
  • Scrub kitchen counters, sink, and exterior appliances
  • Clean and disinfect all bathrooms
  • Empty trash and reline bins
  • Wipe mirrors, windows sills, and light switches
  • Make beds and straighten common areas

Deep Cleaning

The deep clean goes where standard cleaning doesn't. It's ideal for your first booking with us, after a long period between cleans, or when you simply want to reset your home to its very best. Every corner, every surface — done.

  • Everything in our Standard Clean, plus:
  • Clean inside oven, microwave, and refrigerator
  • Scrub grout lines in bathrooms and kitchens
  • Wipe down cabinet faces and drawer fronts
  • Dust baseboards, door frames, and vents
  • Clean behind and underneath large appliances
  • Detailed window interior cleaning
  • Sanitize high-touch areas throughout the home
