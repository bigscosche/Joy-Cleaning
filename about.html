Our Story

A Family Business,
Built from Our Own Home.

We're Maria and Carlos Rivera. We started Joy Cleaning in 2018 because we couldn't find a service we'd trust in our own home. So we built one — and we've been cleaning for our neighbors ever since.

Who We Are

A Small Team.
One Simple Rule.

Clean every home like it's our own.

That's it. We started small — evenings and weekends while Carlos kept his day job — and word spread because we showed up, did the work, and actually answered the phone.

Six years later, we're still doing it ourselves. Every person on our team was trained by us, background-checked by us, and introduced to clients personally. No apps, no staffing agencies, no revolving door.
