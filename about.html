Our Story

We Started Joy Cleaning
Because We Needed It Ourselves.

Maria and Carlos Rivera tried four different cleaning services before launching their own. Too many no-shows. Too many homes left half-done. They knew Danbury deserved better — so in 2018, they built it.

Who We Are

Six Years In. Still Doing It Ourselves.

Joy Cleaning is Maria and Carlos Rivera, their small in-house team, and a single rule: clean every home like it's your own.

We started with just the two of us, cleaning homes evenings and weekends while Carlos kept his day job. Word spread because we actually showed up, actually cleaned thoroughly, and actually answered the phone. We still do.

We've grown since 2018, but we've stayed deliberate about it. Every person who joins our team is trained in-house, background-checked, and vetted personally by us — not through an app, not through a staffing agency. When our team is in your home, they're our team. That matters.

How We Think About Cleaning

The Things We Won't Compromise On

🤝

We Show Up. Every Time.

A cleaning service that cancels last-minute isn't a service — it's a liability. We don't cancel without rescheduling. We don't send strangers without notice. You deserve to rely on us.

✨

Clean Means the Whole Room

The baseboards. The grout. The corner of the shower no one wants to touch. We work to a checklist, and the checklist is thorough. Skipping the hard parts isn't cleaning — it's theater.

🌿

No Products We Wouldn't Use at Home

We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaners as our default — not as an upsell. We have kids. We have pets. We know what it means to live in a home, not just clean it.

This Is Our Town Too

We shop here, go to school here, and wave to neighbors here. Every home we clean belongs to someone in our community. That's not a marketing line — it's a responsibility.
