What Each Visit Includes

A Thorough Clean. Every Time.

Recurring visits aren't a lighter version of our service — they're a full clean of all key areas. We adapt our checklist to your home and preferences over time.

🍳

Kitchen

Countertops, stovetop, sink, exterior appliances, cabinet fronts, and floors. Everything you touch daily, cleaned thoroughly every visit.

🚿

Bathrooms

Toilets, sinks, showers, tubs, mirrors, and floors. Surfaces disinfected, fixtures polished, floors mopped to the edges.

🛋️

Living Areas

All surfaces dusted, floors vacuumed or mopped, furniture addressed. Consistent attention to the rooms your family uses most.

🛏️

Bedrooms

Dusting, vacuuming, and linen changes (if requested). Baseboards and surfaces kept clean between your deeper seasonal visits.
