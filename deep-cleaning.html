What We Do

Every Surface. Every Corner. No Exceptions.

A deep clean is different from your regular maintenance visit. It's the kind of clean that addresses what's been building up in the places you rarely touch — and the ones you wish someone would. Most clients book a deep clean as their first visit with us, then convert to recurring service once they see the difference.

What's Included

Room-by-Room, Top to Bottom

Every deep clean covers all of the following. Nothing is optional or subject to "we'll get to it next time."

🍳

Kitchen

  • Interior of oven, microwave, and refrigerator
  • All cabinet fronts and interiors
  • Stovetop grates and drip pans
  • Countertops, backsplash, and sink
  • Under and behind major appliances
  • Floors mopped edge to edge
🚿

Bathrooms

  • Grout scrubbed by hand
  • Shower doors polished streak-free
  • Hard water stains removed from fixtures
  • Toilet cleaned inside, outside, and behind
  • Vanity drawers and cabinet interiors
  • Exhaust fans dusted
🛏️

Bedrooms

  • Baseboards wiped down throughout
  • Ceiling fans and light fixtures dusted
  • Under-bed vacuumed
  • Window sills and tracks cleaned
  • Closet shelves wiped
  • All surfaces, mirrors, and doors
🏠

Living Areas

  • Vents and air returns vacuumed
  • Blinds and window treatments dusted
  • Upholstered furniture vacuumed thoroughly
  • Walls spot-cleaned where needed
  • Floors vacuumed and mopped
  • All door frames and switch plates wiped
Who It's For

You Probably Need a Deep Clean If...

🆕

First-Time Clients

Most new clients start with a deep clean. It establishes a baseline so recurring visits are faster, more thorough, and more effective long-term.

🗓️

Seasonal Resets

Spring or fall — twice a year is the standard. Deep cleaning twice yearly keeps your home from accumulating the buildup that regular visits can't address.

📦

Before or After a Move

Moving in or out? A deep clean ensures every surface is addressed — ideal for making a strong impression or starting fresh in your new space.

🎉

Post-Event or Post-Construction

After a party, renovation, or period of heavy use, a deep clean resets everything. We handle the aftermath so you don't have to.
